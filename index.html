About Us

SERVING THE INDIAN ARMED FORCES.

D’SUTRA Research and Consulting Services Pvt. Ltd. (D’Sutra) is a team of trusted group of individuals very passionate about providing supplies and services to the Air Force and Defense services.

We operate as an aggregator and conglomerate to augment military wide sourcing, services and supplies, with the purpose to fill the gaps in the logistics the armed force's bases face - especially at the forward and remote locations. Our team consists of highly experienced and networked ex-servicemen/veterans and civilians with experience in sourcing, supplying, servicing, technology, reverse engineering and executing, handholding and delivering projects across a wide gamete – even in war zones overseas working alongside NATO & Multi National Forces.


We bring a combined wealth of experience from within the Armed forces and the corporate world, nationally and internationally.


With our experience and global reach strengthened by a team of highly experienced, intelligent and innovative visionary team of advisors and consultants from across the globe with a common goal towards the benifit of India - we are able to table an effective professional approach with personal touch to every project we work on, which is why the military keeps time and again entrusting us with their peripheral jobs while they can focus on their core responsibilities.


"To deliver to the Indian forces - the best of the solutions available globally, indigenized at the best possible value with no compromise on quality, performance or standards."
